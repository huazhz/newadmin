Why IMEDECS?

When it comes to independent medical reviews, you want accurate, timely results without hassle. And that’s what we’ve been doing for decades.

In fact, Independent Medical Expert Consulting Services, Inc. (IMEDECS) was known as Hayes Plus back in 1998. At that time, we were one of the initial, eight companies acting as betas when the original accreditation standards were written.

Since then, we have established a reputation in the national healthcare industry for providing the highest quality, independent medical review products and services available.

But with IMEDECS, you don’t just get objective and comprehensive medical review services from experts who have been accredited since the beginning. You get much more…

Enjoy the unique advantages of working with IMEDECS:

  • Reduce stress and comply with ease.

    We are active members of the National Association of Independent Review Organizations (NAIRO). And our National Committee for Quality Assurance (NCQA) and Utilization Review Accreditation Commission (URAC) accreditations ensure compliance to the industry’s most stringent protocols with respect to internal and external review procedures, quality assurance, confidentiality, and security.

    In fact, we are on the URAC standards committee, attend meetings in Washington, D.C. every year and have regular phone conversations with URAC to stay constantly updated.

  • Work with the most experienced panel of healthcare specialists in the industry.

    Because of our long-standing reputation, IMEDECS attracts the highest quality physicians in numerous, areas of medical expertise. Specialists nationwide come to IMEDECS for exciting and unique review opportunities.

    This means that our physician panel includes unsurpassed quality, depth and breadth to handle whatever requests we receive. (In fact, our competitors often ask us for medical professionals to help fulfill their review obligations!)

  • Meet deadlines with cutting-edge technology.

    Working with our experts, you will get solutions that meet your time frames… whether it’s immediate or a few months from now. Plus, you can take advantage of the latest innovations with AccessIMEDECS.

    Our state-of-the-art online review management system is accessible to you twenty-four hours a day, seven days a week

  • Enjoy talking to a real human being and unmatched customer service.

    To give you the best possible results in a timely manner, we believe that communication and respect are key. That’s why we talk to you directly to discuss your specific needs and are available to answer your questions.

Our mission is to provide independent, informed expert medical review services that resolve coverage disputes or evaluate quality of care. Through our medical review program, we are dedicated to quality health care and improved clinical outcomes.
